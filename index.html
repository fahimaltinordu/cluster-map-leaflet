Enable Geolocation

iOS (iPhone, iPad)

  • Open your iOS's Settings app.
  • Tap [Privacy] > [Location Services] Make sure that Location Services is ON.
  • Tap [Privacy] > [Location Services] > [Safari Websites]
  • Tap [Allow Location Access] > Set to [While Using the App]
  • After these steps click the "Refresh" button

Android

  • Swipe down from the status bar to open the notification panel, then swipe down further to show all shortcut switches. Touch to location icon turn it on.
  • Go to Settings > Location, and enable Access my location.
  • After these steps click the "Refresh" button
